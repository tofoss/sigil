Quick Stir-Fried Greens
A fast and flavorful stir-fried greens dish using soy sauce, garlic, and chili. Great as a side or a light meal.
Prep time: 15 minutes
Serves 2

Ingredients:
- 200g bok choy
- 1-2 tablespoons soy sauce (adjust to taste)
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2-1 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add garlic and chili flakes (if using), sauté briefly until fragrant.
3. Add bok choy and stir-fry until wilted, about 2-3 minutes.
4. Add soy sauce and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
